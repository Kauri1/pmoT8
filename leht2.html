LoL as an eSport

Since its debut in 2009, League of Legends has evolved from a small population of desktop-computer warriors into a full-scale phenomenon. In the process, it has become an e-sport. Though e-sports were around for about a decade before Riot Games was born, no company has jumped in with the same intensity.

Riot controls every aspect of the professional league, right down to the music composed for live events.
