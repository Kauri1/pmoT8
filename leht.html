League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five face off to destroy the other teams base. The Nexus is the heart of both teams’ bases. To win the game, a team must destroy the enemy’s Nexus first. (Riot Games Limited, kuupäev puudub)
The game was released on 27th of October 2009. The game is both developed and produced by Riot Games. The game is categorized under the action, and the MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) genres. (IGN Entertainment, kuupäev puudub)
